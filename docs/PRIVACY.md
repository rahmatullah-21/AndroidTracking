# Privacy Policy — Device Insight Pro (template)

_Last updated: {DATE}. This is a template — review with legal counsel before publishing._

Device Insight Pro ("the app") helps you understand how **your own device** is used. It is intended
for the device owner or a person they have explicitly authorized (e.g. a parent configuring a
family device). The app is a transparency and digital-wellbeing tool and must not be used to monitor
another adult without their knowledge and consent.

## What data the app accesses

- **App usage statistics** (via Usage Access): foreground time, launch counts, last-used times,
  and screen sessions, used to build usage and screen-time analytics.
- **Notifications** (only if you enable Notification Access): the posting app, the title, a **short
  text preview**, a category, and the timestamp. Full message bodies, images and attachments are
  **not** stored.
- **Social / messaging previews** (same Notification Access): for recognized chat apps (WhatsApp,
  Instagram, Messenger, Telegram, Snapchat, Signal, etc.) the app records the **sender name and the
  message preview that appears in the notification**, plus the platform and timestamp. It reads only
  what the operating system shows in notifications — it does **not** access the messaging app's
  private message database, and it does not log keystrokes or scrape the screen.
- **Device events**: screen on/off, unlock, charging, Wi‑Fi/Bluetooth/headphone state, battery-low,
  and reboot — for the activity timeline.
- **Network usage**: device-level Wi‑Fi and mobile data totals.
- **Battery & performance**: battery level/health/temperature/voltage, RAM/storage/process counts.
- **Installed apps & their permissions**: to compute the security score (read-only; the app never
  changes other apps' permissions).

## Where data is stored

All of the above is stored **locally on your device** in an app-private database. It is excluded
from cloud backup and device-transfer by default. Nothing is transmitted off the device unless you
explicitly enable the optional **cloud sync** feature.

## Cloud sync (optional, off by default)

If you enable cloud sync, summary reports and settings are synchronized to your authenticated
Firebase account so you can view them across your own devices. You can disable sync and delete
cloud data at any time.

## Data retention & deletion

Local monitoring data is automatically pruned after the configured retention window (default 90
days). You can clear all data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling it.

## Permissions you control

Usage Access, Notification Access, and the background monitoring service are all **opt-in** and can
be revoked at any time in Android Settings or in the app's Settings screen.
